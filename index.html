About Us

Welcome to KA29 Travels – Your Local Travel Companion!

At KA29 Travels, we specialize in providing reliable and comfortable cab services for city tours, outstation trips, and personalized travel packages. Based in Bagalkot, we are proud to serve our community with safe, affordable, and timely travel solutions.

Whether you're planning a family vacation, a temple visit, or a one-way trip, our experienced drivers and well-maintained vehicles ensure a smooth journey every time. We believe in quality service, honest pricing, and customer satisfaction.
